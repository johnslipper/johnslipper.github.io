Salt Wise is a fun online quiz commissioned by the Stroke Foundation New Zealand to spread awareness about salt in our day-to-day diet.

The project is built on the Twitter Bootstrap framework for it's useful JavaScript components and also extends a great JQuery quiz plugin to include HTML5 sound using Buzz and Facebook score sharing.

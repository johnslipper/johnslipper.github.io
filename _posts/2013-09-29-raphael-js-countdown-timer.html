During work on the Stroke Wise online quiz I had to research methods of creating a circular countdown timer.

The timer had to also support legacy versions of Internet Explorer back as far as 8 so I decided Raphaël JS was the most obvious choice as it both uses newer SVG markup for modern browsers while also providing legacy Internet Explorer support through their proprietary VML engine.

This demo shows how a simple countdown timer can be implemented, including a button to increment the time as it is counting down.
